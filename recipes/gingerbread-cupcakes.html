Gingerbread Cupcakes

Abi Porter, CC BY 2.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

Easy to whip together, these dark, full-bodied cupcakes are moist and tender. They're especially nice with a little dollop of non-dairy whipped cream on top!

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter or oil
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsulphered molasses
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 1/4 cups unbleached white flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350° F and generously butter the baking tin (or use paper liners)
  2. Cream the butter or oil and sugar until light with an electric mixer
  3. Add the molasses and egg and beat until smooth
  4. Add the ginger, cinnamon, allspice, nutmeg, and flour and beat until well blended
  5. In a separate cup, dissolve the baking soda in the boiling water
  6. Add the baking soda and boiling water to the batter and mix until smooth
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared muffin tins
  8. Bake for 20 minutes, or until the cupcakes are slightly springy to the touch
  9. Cool for 2 to 3 minutes, and then remove the cupcakes from the tin and cool on racks
