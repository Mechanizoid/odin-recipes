Banana Bread

By Famartin - Own work, CC BY-SA 4.0, Link

Description

This is classic home recipe for delicious healthy banana bread! When I was a child, my mom made this recipe often, and it always reminds me of my childhood home. For sweeter breads, I recommend using very ripe (almost rotten) bananas.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 cups mashed bananas
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1/2 cup of applesauce
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of organic raw sugar
  • 3 tsp vanilla
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of whole wheat pastry flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp cinnamon and nutmeg
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
  • 1/2 cup of chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Grease two loaf pans and preheat the overn to 350° F
  2. Mash the bananas and set them aside.
  3. Sift the flour, baking soda, and spices together.
  4. In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs, oil, and applesauce together.
  5. Add the sugar and vanilla to the egg and oil mixture and beat until smooth.
  6. Add the flour mix to the wet ingredients and beat well. The batter will be stiff.
  7. Fold the banana mash into the batter.
  8. If you are adding nuts, fold them into the mix now.
  9. Divide the batter into the two baking pans, and sprinkle chocolate chips on top if you like.
  10. Bake for 45 minutes to an hour, or until a toothpick inserted into the middle comes out clean.
